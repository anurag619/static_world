Best Quora reads this week

Sat 06 December 2014

Ok, so as the title suggests I have started this series of posts from quora. Now why would I do that? Well, staraight forward answer I wish to collect these awesome reads that comes across my Quora profile and I wanted a new topic to write about.

What was it like to grow up in India in the 1990s I am sure if you are a true Quora reader you would have seen this question many times. I bet you will smile reading the answer, only if you were born in the 90s.

What are the biggest misconceptions that Indians have about India - Well, if you were born in an average middle class family, this answer needs no explanation. Speaking of which, I am glad I will never join that league.

What are some of the best travel and exploring places in India which are not overcrowded with tourists - This answer prompted me to start this series in the first place. The post is bit longer I agree but she had to cover all on her list. I have visited few of these and hoping I will visit them in near future.

I am hearing the packages of 1 crores and 75 lakhs these days in IIT campus placements Where are they made to do their work in the US or India - I have no words for this, the answer explains it all.

If an IIT graduate does not get a job in the campus placements what are his options after graduation - Again a post worth mentioning, brave heart and bold decision. He is my also profile to be followed this week.

What is it like being raised by relatively uneducated parents parents who have no graduate degree and arent in a business of any kind but earn a nominal salary - A inspiring post which makes me realize how sometimes we take things for granted and do not value them.
